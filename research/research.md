**<div align="center">Big questions I am interested in:</div>**

[**Genetics**](genetics.md): (1) Can we use people's DNA to help identify individuals who are at high risk risk for diseases that could benefit from early intervention? (2) Can we identify locations in the DNA that are causally linked to complex human traits? (3) Can we use genetics to help equalize healthcare inequalities?

[**Physics**](physics.md): (1) Can we model confinement? (a rigorous answer to this question that incorporates what is known about the Standard Model is one of the [Milenium Problems](https://www.claymath.org/millennium-problems/yang%E2%80%93mills-and-mass-gap) (2) How can we describe the non-perturbative physics of quarks and gluons in high energy limits? (Regge Physics, saturation, quark gluon plasma, etc.) (3) How can we better describe conformal field theory which appears in lots of physical systems that exhibit strange phase changing behavior.
